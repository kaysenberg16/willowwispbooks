The House in the Cerulean Sea

TJ Klune

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Fantasy · Cozy · Found Family

This is the book I recommend more than any other. It's warm, quietly powerful, and full of characters that feel like old friends by the end. If you've ever felt like an outsider, this one's for you. I keep multiple copies in the trailer at all times because I press it into everyone's hands.

Book cover

Legends & Lattes

Travis Baldree

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Fantasy · Cozy · Slice of Life

An orc barbarian retires from adventuring to open a coffee shop. That's the whole pitch and it's perfect. Low stakes, high warmth. This is the book equivalent of a rainy afternoon with a latte and nowhere to be.

Book cover

The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue

V.E. Schwab

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Fantasy · Historical · Romance

A girl makes a deal with the darkness and is cursed to be forgotten by everyone she meets. Three hundred years of living without being remembered — until someone does. The prose is gorgeous and the ending wrecked me. One of the most beautiful books I've ever read.
