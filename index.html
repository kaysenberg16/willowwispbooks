Willow Wisp Books is a roaming trailer & popup bookstore bringing stories, charm, and quiet magic to community events.

Find Us at an Event

Willow Wisp Books travels to markets, festivals, and gatherings with a carefully curated selection of books and bookish goods. Each stop is shaped by place, season, and community. No two pop-ups are exactly the same! Our home base is Maple Valley, WA, but we definitely do some traveling!
