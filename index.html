About

Willow Wisp Books travels to markets, festivals, and gatherings with a carefully curated selection of books and bookish goods. Each stop is shaped by place, season, and community — no two pop-ups are exactly the same!

Our home base is Maple Valley, WA, but we definitely do some traveling!

Books are where we go to find the people and places to whom we belong.

What You'll Find
